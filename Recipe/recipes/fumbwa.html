Fumbwa

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cupt water
  • 11 ounces (300 grams) baby spinach, finely chopped
  • 3 spring onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • ETC

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Bring water to a simmer in a large pot and add the spinach
  3. Once they have reduced in volume by about half, add the spring onions, garlic, and tomatoes and continue to simmer. Crumble the chicken stock cube into the pot and mix well.
  4. Make sure all bones and skin have been removed from the smoked fish, then add them to the post. Allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add 3 to 4 tablespoons of red palm oil into the pot.
